Event Details

  • This is a group competition with a maximum of 2 members per group.
  • Teams have to present a poster based on the given topics.

  • Entry Fee : Rs. 100/Person (Rs.200/team)

Rules

  • Poster should be original and hand made.
  • The poster should be of minimum A3 size.
  • College ID is compulsary (digital format will not be accepted)
  • Final Decisions will be made by the event organisers.
  • Any malpractice shall lead to disqualification of the team.
