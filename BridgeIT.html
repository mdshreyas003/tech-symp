CIVIL DEPARTMENT

BRIGE-IT

"Design. Test. Redesign."

Event Details

  • This is a group competition with minimum of 1 and maximum of 2 Participants per team.
  • The competition will be held in only one round.
  • Participants will have to prepare a model bridge with the help of provided material.
  • Ratio of the load carried by the bridge and the self weight will be considered.
  • Entry Fee : Rs. 100 per Person

Rules

  • The model should be prepared only with the help of fevicol and sticks provided the organisers
  • Model should be prepared as per given dimensions.
  • College ID is compulsary (digital format will not be accepted)
  • Final Decisions will be made by the event organisers.
  • Any malpractice shall lead to disqualification of the team.
